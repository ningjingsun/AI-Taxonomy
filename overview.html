Compare



Definition:

Compare entities within a corpus of similar entities. Organize a collection of entities based on intention to maximize or minimize some outcome.


Synonyms:

Rank, Order, Find Best, Find Fastest, Find Cheapest, Recommend


Examples:

Rank a set of products based on the liklihood a customer might want to buy them. Generate a stream of socialmedia posts to maximize the time a customer spends looking at the media.



Compare the best routes Compare likely next word for partial sentence


Compare impact of resource distribution schedules

Compose



Definition:

Generate something new (message, image, sound) based on knowledge of similar things.


Synonyms:

Make, Generate, Construct, Author


Examples:

Answer this person's question. Make the agent sound like they are a teenager from France. Remove the chimey and fill in the image detail from where it was removed.



Compose the missing or damaged parts of existing images


Compose the driving path Compose text from speech (video content)

Detect



Definition:

Notice if a specific kind of a thing is in a data set or if it shows up in a senosr stream.


Synonyms:

Monitor, Sense, Notice, Classify, Discriminate


Examples:

Does the audio contain a human voice? Does the user's motion indicate that they are walking? Is there a face in this image? Does anything in the image look like a hand?



Detect emotion in voice audio Detect defects in product image


Detect lane boundaries in image

Discover



Definition:

Analyze a dataset and notice a pattern that allows clustering of similar things.


Synonyms:

Extract, Notice, Organize, Cluster, Group, Connect, Reveal


Examples:

What are the different ways users use this web site? Sort these people into groups based on things they seem to like.



Discover new fraudulent tactics Discover new uses for drug


Discover defect in product image

Estimate



Definition:

Infer a value (ex: location, position, size, grade, chance of something being true) related to the current situation.


Synonyms:

Rate, Grade, Measure, Assess


Examples:

How long it will take to reach your destination if you left right now? What are the chances the message you are viewing is spam? What is the direction of the speaker from the microphone?



Estimate risk of fraud Estimate drug toxicity Estimate travel time

Forecast



Definition:

Infer a future value (stock price, sales, weather, chance of something being true) based on current conditions and history. Infer a peak or valley in a trend line (minimum or maximum).


Synonyms:

Predict, Guess, Speculate


Examples:

When is the best time to buy a specific stock? What will the weather be tomorrow? How much can I sell my home for in a year?



Forecast crop yield Forecast likelihood of system breakdown


Forecast usage peaks and valleys

Identify



Definition:

Notice if a specific item or class of items shows up in a set of like times.


Synonyms:

Recognize, Discern, Find, Notice


Examples:

Does this image of a face show your friend Mary? Is this message span or not spam? What kind of cat is that?



Identify anomalies as tumors or not Identify emotion in face image


Identify key data points from documents

This AI Taxonomy aims to support professional product designers, UX designers and startups to better envision and prototype new products and services that integate AI capabilities.

Each card represents a task that AI could do. You can sort through the cards by selecting “by Capability” or "by Industry" at the top right bar. You can also use filters on the left side to filter cards, and click any card to view the workflow in details.
