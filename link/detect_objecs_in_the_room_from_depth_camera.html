AR Item Viewing


Allow users to preview furniture and other home products in their own physical environment when doing online shopping. Through smartphone cameras, it detects the room, objects in the room, and collision of virtual products and the objects in the room to place single product or multiple products to the room, which helps consumers visualize how a product or a whole set of products could fit together in their own space. It can help users make shopping decisions, and increase sales for e-commerce platforms, brands, or merchants.


Industry: Commerce & Sales
Service Type: B2C

Sequences

1A.Detect the room from depth camera  >  2.Detect objects in the room from depth camera  >  3.Detect spatial characteristics and size  >  4.Estimate space between the objects from depth camera  >  5.Detect collision events of digital product and objects in space    
1B.Identify objects with depth camera    

Supervised Machine Learning

Input Data:


An image from the depth camera

Output Data:


Label saying if there are objects or not

Data Features:


Texture/outline shape of the light dots
