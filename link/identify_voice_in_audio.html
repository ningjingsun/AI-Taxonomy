Biometric Security


Biometric Security is a security mechanism that first detects the users' biological features such as voice and fingerprint and then compares the detect features with the features in the database. The verification process helps determine the identify of people by authenticating their non-transferrable physical characteristics and thus ensure safe access to important information. This feature can improve protection against unauthorized users and prevent information leakage. It can also make access to personal accounts faster, more safely and seamlessly. This feature can encrypt and provide secure access to the private information of users by verifying and ensuring the identity of users.
