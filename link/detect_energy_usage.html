Energy & Utility Management


Energy & Utility Management presents the user with information of utilities and energy use commercially or in a home. This feature can provide information about peak usage, cost saving opportunities, repairs that may need to be done, ideal temperatures, and energy/utility usage. This feature can drill down into causes of abnormal energy use by predicting special needs of energy use and can formulate new services and energy use programs.
