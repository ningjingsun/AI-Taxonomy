Monitoring Crop Health


Crop health monitoring is a smart farming management technique that could monitor the condition of crops and estimate the growth of crops by detecting and analyzing any abnormal signs of stress or other environmental factors. This feature can reduce the human labor and time labor required to monitor the crops manually.


Industry: Manufacturing, Production & Agriculture
Service Type: B2C

Sequences

1A. Detect signs of stress on crops  >  2A. Identify source of crop stress
1B. Estimate growth of current crop  >  2B. Forecast crop yield
1C. Estimate soil health
1D. Compare impact of resource distribution schedules    

Supervised Machine Learning

Input Data:


Datasets of current crops; datasets of past crops and past crop yield

Output Data:


A number indicating the crop yield in the future

Data Features:


Leaf area, leaf orientation, leaf spatial arrangement, leaf roughness, chlorophyll content, canopy temperature, season, geographical location, weather, precipitation
