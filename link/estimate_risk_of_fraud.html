Fraudulent Transaction Detection


Fraudulent Transaction Detection is a security technique that finds patterns of fraud and identifies potental fraudulent transactions in order to prevent unauthorized financial activity. This feature can enable proactive fraud prevention to protect the money and assets of customers. This feature can help financial institutions to sustain the goodwill from the customer, and reduce cost of customer services and case investigations.


Industry: Risk Mitigation & Security
Service Type: B2C

Sequences

1A. Identify safe and fraudulent transactions  >  2. Estimate risk of fraud
1B. Discover new fraudulent tactics

Supervised Machine Learning

Input Data:


Dataset of fraudulent transections

Output Data:


Fraud risk score saying the severity of the fraud under examination

Data Features:


Worst-case financial lost, fraud type, transaction value, transaction time, transaction location, merchant name, card type, device type, IP data
