Energy & Utility Management


Energy & Utility Management presents the user with information of utilities and energy use commercially or in a home. This feature can provide information about peak usage, cost saving opportunities, repairs that may need to be done, ideal temperatures, and energy/utility usage. This feature can drill down into causes of abnormal energy use by predicting special needs of energy use and can formulate new services and energy use programs.


Industry: Infrastructure & Utilities
Service Type: B2B

Sequences

1. Detect energy usage  >  2. Forecast usage peaks and valleys  >  3. Compare impact of temperature settings (optimal)  >  4. Forecast infrastructure part will breakdown    

Supervised Machine Learning

Input Data:


Dataset of energy usage, dataset of maintenance, dataset of previous breakdowns

Output Data:


Score and labels of impact, visual and audio cue when the impact is hazardous

Data Features:


Degrees of breakdown, levels of part's endurance
