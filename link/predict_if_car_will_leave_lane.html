AV Lane Departure


AV Lane Departure alerts the driver of a vehicle when they may be unintentionally leaving their lane. This feature can minimize avoidable car accidents. This feature can make vehicle company to make other selling option and can help autonomous driving company to generate stabilize mechanisms. Also this feature can create new environment for driving industry and thus create new revenues.


Industry: Transportation
Service Type: B2C

Sequences

1. Detect lane boundaries in image  >  2. Estimate car's position within lane  >  3. Forecast if car will leave lane  >  4. Identify intention to depart or not depart lane  >  5. Detect if nearby objects might hit  >  6. Estimate danger from lane departure    

Supervised Machine Learning

Input Data:


Sensor stream coming form video, featurization

Output Data:


Label saying whether car will leave the lane or not (audio or visual cues)

Data Features:


Prospect car position (location and angle), distance between lanes and car, speed per distance of car
