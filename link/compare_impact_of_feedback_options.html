Feedback Systems


Feedback Systems provides users to maximize student-teacher interaction and feedback while minimizing time it takes for teacherse to give students feedback. This feature can help instructors reducing time for documentation and be aware of students. Therefore, increase engagement with their students. This feature can make automatic system for institutions to minimize time of gathering student's information and to deliver students balanced and impartial results.
