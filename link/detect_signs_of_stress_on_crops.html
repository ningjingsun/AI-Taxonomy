Monitoring Crop Health


Crop health monitoring is a smart farming management technique that could monitor the condition of crops as well as critical environmental factors. This feature can reduce the human labor required to monitor crops. This feature can also reduce the high costs required to monitor crops.


Industry: Manufacturing, Production & Agriculture
Service Type: B2C

Sequences

1A. Detect signs of stress on crops  >  2A. Identify source of crop stress
1B. Estimate growth of current crop  >  2B. Forecast crop yield
1C. Estimate soil health
1D. Compare impact of resource distribution schedules    

Supervised Machine Learning

Input Data:


Datasets with labels indicating whether there is stress on crops or not

Output Data:


Stress score indicating the level of stress on crops

Data Features:


Leaf area, leaf orientation, leaf spatial arrangement, leaf roughness, chlorophyll content, canopy temperature
