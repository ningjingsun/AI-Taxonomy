New Medical Data Synthesis


To overcome the paucity of annotated medical data in real-world settings, new medical data sythesis is a commonly used technique to generate synthetic health records from publicy available data for the purpose of testing theories, data models, algorithms, or prototyping medical innovations. This feature could generate health data that protect the individual patient's privacy, while providing researchers with valuable abundant data to conduct testing and research. Synthetic data could increase the quantity and diversity of existing medical datasets, and therefore improve the accuracy of medical data models and encourage more medical discovery in the field.
