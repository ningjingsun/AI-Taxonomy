HR Chatbot


HR Chatbot assists employess in their workplaces by answering frequent questions about their work. It can also identify questions, compose best answers and recommend what are frequent questions they receive. This feature supports employees to access information about job duties, processes, trainings, filing claims and etc. Sometimes it can lessen emotional efforts and can save time to find solutions that they don't know where to start. This feature can increase the efficiency of work and minimize the time employees spend on human resource processes. Therefore, it can help company to increase productivity of work and produce work Q&A or work manuals for the future based on the frequent question they receive.
