Drug Discovery & Development


Drug discovery and development is the process by which new candidate medications are discovered through clinical trials and post-approval monitoring. By analyzing the drug's gene sequences, the researchers can make predictions about a drug's possible protein structures, phycio-chmical reactions and bioactivity, and thus better evaluate the promise of a drug. The trained models enable medical researchers to analyze a drug's structures and promise faster and with higher accuracy. Through this process, new medicines can be devloped and released to the market, therefore solve the disease and improve the well-beings of patients as well as create new revenues for the pharmacy industries.
