Review Analytics


Scanning customer reviews for hospitalities to discover similar expressions/topics that discussed in the reviews and classify them by identifying users' sentiment and intent. This can help make informed decisions on the service and operational level to improve the reviews. It can promote more relevant and qualified suggestions for users by suggesting changes to the hospitality providers (It's really difficult to do by hand (accelerator) but also an enhancer as they get insights they would never get), and help hospitalities save cost on review analysis and discover problems.
