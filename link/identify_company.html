Media Stream


Presents the user with a list of media elements they can select from or that might autoplay. Users can often explicitly indicate content or entities that they like as a way of interacting with the system. The AI system most often attempts to maximize one or more specific user behaviors. For example, many services want users to click on ads interleaved within the media. They often also want users to dwell longer, a sign of engagement as well as an opportunity for the user to encounter more ads. It can provide users with more interesting and desirable content, and increases ad revenue.
