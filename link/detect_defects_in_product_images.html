Predictive Maintenance


Defect detection is commonly used in manufacturing process for manufacturing quality control and assurance before market release. By detecting abnomalies in product images and compare features of abnomalies against known types of defects, this feature can identify the types of defects of products and forecast likelihood of defects. This feature can reduce the human labor and time labor required to detect the defections manually. This feature can monitor quality controls of manufactured products and therefore reduce the costs of defect components and customer services.

Input Data:


Product images with labels indicating whether there are defects or not

Output Data:


Label saying if the product image has defects or not

Data Features:


Location of defects, width of defects, height of defects, depth of defects
