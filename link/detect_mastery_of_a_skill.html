Personalized Lesson Plans


Personalized Lesson Plans provides adaptive learning, individualized learning, differentiated learning, and competence-based learning. This feature allows students to achieve the best learning outcomes by tailoring courseworks their own learning process. This feature can lead institutions to make automating learning programs, delivering personalized lessons to students who do not attend class. Therefore, it can accelerate the expansion of the field of education and the generalization of private tutoring.
