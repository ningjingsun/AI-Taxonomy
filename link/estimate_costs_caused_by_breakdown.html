Predictive Maintenance


Predictive maintenance is a technique that helps determine the condition of in-service equipment based on factors such as the lifespans and remaining energy of machines in order to estimate when maintenance should be performed. This feature can reduce the human labor and time labor required to monitor the equipment manually. By predicting when maintenance will be required, this technique can achieve "just-in-time" in manufacturing, improve productivity and reduce the costs and likelihood of failures in equipment.
