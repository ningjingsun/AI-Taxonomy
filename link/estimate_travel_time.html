Best Routes (GPS)


Best Route (GPS) provides a user with the most efficient route to a destination based on the provided user and driving constraints. This feature can minimize unnecessary driving time, in traffic, or in other undesirable conditions. This feature can avoid lost time and fuel due to congestion and ensures faster delivery times. It can also help traffic authority by revealing new potential routes.


Industry: Transportation
Service Type: B2C
